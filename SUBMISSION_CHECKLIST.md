# Submission Checklist

- Finalize App Store name, description, subtitle, and keywords in App Store Connect.
- Add privacy policy URL and support URL.
- Prepare App Store screenshots for required iPhone sizes.
- Confirm the production 1024x1024 app icon is set.
- Write App Review notes that explain camera/photo-library usage and core scan flow.
- Confirm account management and account deletion requirements are satisfied if user accounts remain part of the app experience.
- Verify the final iOS bundle identifier, signing team, provisioning, and capabilities in Xcode.
- Confirm the latest required Xcode version and iOS SDK before upload.
- Run a final TestFlight smoke test: launch, scan, manual search, details, collection save, and auth flows.
